<h3>Annotation data</h3>
<p>Annotation data-tab contains a data table consisting of variations in your input set, general information about the variations (allele, chromosomal location), and association of the variations to annotations as a binary table (0 means no association, 1 means an association). The data can be downloaded through the “Download data” button. The downloaded data can be further analyzed with external tools.
</p>
